Check - In: Transfer Tote / Universal Pallets

- Inbound Dropoff

  • Verfiy the Trailer License Plate and VRID on GEM
  • Radio Inbound and say "Inbound, I have a Transfer Totes or Universal Pallet Pickup, they're on their way to 135C"
  • Assign the Driver a Yard Slip between 463 - 534
  • After verfiying the necessary information, assign the Driver either a Dead Slip (DS108 - DS124) or a Yard Slip between 320 - 381
  • Enter the VRID, Reason and Information tom-inbound Slack chat

    • VRID 115H9BS5J

    TransfersTotes

    Driver heading to 135C


  • After informing Inbound, tell the driver to park across from and walk into 135C. They will recieve their paperwork and be told where to pick up their Trailer inside

    • Note: Sometimes the Driver does not recieve paperwork at 135C and you will need to print it out when they arrive at the Exit Gate
